Privacy Policy

DailyPromise — Bible Verse & Devotional · Last updated: July 6, 2026

DailyPromise ("we," "our," or "the app") is a Christian Bible verse and devotional app published by ClearPass Technologies. We built DailyPromise to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. DailyPromise has no user accounts and no sign-up. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your app preferences — such as saved and bookmarked verses, reading history, notification settings, and subscription status — are stored locally on your device only, using your device's standard app storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Subscriptions & Purchases

DailyPromise Premium is offered as an auto-renewing subscription processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details. Purchase and subscription status is managed by Apple and validated on your device. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy for how Apple handles transactions.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device by the app. No notification data is sent to us or any third party.

Content Delivery

The app loads some visual assets (such as background imagery) from a content delivery network. These requests are standard web requests to fetch images and are not used to identify or track you.

Third-Party Services

DailyPromise does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. We do not sell or share any data, because we do not collect any.

Children's Privacy

DailyPromise does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages and collects no personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@dailypromise.io.
