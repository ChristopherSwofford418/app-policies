Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock Premium?
Open the app, tap any Premium prompt (for example "Unlock Full Devotional"), and choose the Monthly or Annual plan. Purchases are processed securely by Apple through your Apple ID.

How do I restore a purchase?
On the subscription screen, tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your device, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions, or open apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions. You can cancel anytime; access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Do you collect my data?
No. DailyPromise has no accounts and does not collect or transmit personal information. See our Privacy Policy.
